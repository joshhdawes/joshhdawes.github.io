Postdoctoral Research Associate at SnT, Luxembourg

Welcome! Bienvenue!

I'm a Postdoctoral Research Associate at the Interdisciplinary Centre for Security, Reliability and Trust in Luxembourg. My supervisor is Professor Domenico Bianculli and the head of my group is Professor Lionel Briand.

I also have a role as an External Consultant for work stemming from my PhD at the CMS Experiment at CERN in Geneva.

I did my PhD (submitted, awaiting defence) with the University of Manchester while based at CERN. I focused on Runtime Verification for performance analysis of programs. My supervisors were Dr Giles Reger (Manchester), Dr Giovanni Franzoni and Dr Andreas Pfeiffer (CERN).

My work focuses on static, dynamic and offline analysis of various kinds of computer systems. I care about identifying problems that are being faced in practice, developing the theory to solve them, and building systems that are strong enough to be used in the real-world.

To reach me, you can use joshua.dawes at uni.lu or cern.ch. uni.lu is the best option.
