I am...

a Postdoctoral Researcher at SnT, Luxembourg. My role involves leading research activities in Runtime Verification as part of COSMOS, a Horizon 2020 project. A large part of my research involves extending the contributions to the field made by my PhD.

a Scholars Programme Tutor with The Brilliant Club. Engagement with the general public is key. I get involved in science communication and outreach whenever I can.

an External Expert at the CMS Experiment at CERN. My role will involve developing my PhD research inline with CMS needs.

A bit about my PhD - read my Thesis!

Completed with the University of Manchester while based at CERN on the Doctoral Student programme.

Focused on Runtime Verification for performance analysis of programs.

Led a small team in developing of the VyPR framework at CERN, whose underlying theory I developed.

Led to multiple CERN and CMS-funded projects, along with an inter-institute collaboration between CERN, the University of Manchester, and Universita della Svizzera italiana.

Supervisors: Dr Giles Reger (Manchester), Dr Giovanni Franzoni (CERN) and Dr Andreas Pfeiffer (CERN).
